Our Learning Programs

Age-appropriate activities that combine education with hands-on farm experience

Little Sprouts (Ages 2-4)

Gentle introduction to farm life with sensory activities and animal interactions.

  • Animal feeding time
  • Sensory garden exploration
  • Simple nature crafts
  • Story time under trees

Farm Explorers (Ages 4-6)

Interactive learning through farm chores, planting, and caring for animals.

  • Garden planting activities
  • Animal care routines
  • Seasonal learning projects
  • Nature science experiments

Eco Warriors (Ages 6-8)

Advanced farm management and environmental conservation projects.

  • Sustainable farming practices
  • Wildlife conservation projects
  • Leadership activities
  • Advanced science experiments
